Nearby Attraction

Dive into the rich tapestry of Rajasthani culture as you explore the lakes, forts, desert safaris, havelis, and museums. The lively atmosphere surrounding these attractions guarantees an unforgettable experience. Immerse yourself in traditions that have endured the test of time, all conveniently situated just a short distance from Chirag Haveli.

Patwon ki Haveli

A mere 10-minute journey from Chirag Haveli leads you to the architectural brilliance of Patwon ki Haveli. This cluster of five havelis showcases the pinnacle of intricate craftsmanship, a testament to the artistic expressions that define Jaisalmer's heritage. Marvel at the detailed facades, ornate balconies, and the rich history etched into every nook and cranny. Patwon ki Haveli beckons you to step back in time and witness the cultural tapestry that makes it a must-visit in Jaisalmer. Just moments away from our haveli, this historical gem invites you to explore and unravel the secrets of its captivating past.

Sam Sand Dunes

Embark on a desert adventure just a short 5-minute drive from Chirag Haveli, and you'll find yourself amidst the mesmerizing landscape of Sam Sand Dunes. This natural wonder unfolds like a golden carpet, stretching as far as the eye can see. Feel the soft grains of sand beneath your feet and witness the ever-changing hues of the desert as the sun casts its warm glow.

Jaisalmer Fort

Within a mere 2-minute walk from our hotel lies the mesmerizing Golden Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that stands as a testament to the grandeur of Jaisalmer's history. This historic marvel invites you to explore its intricate architecture and narratives of a bygone era. As you step into the shadows of the fort, let the tales of valor and royalty unfold before you. Experience the magic of Jaisalmer Fort, a stone's throw away from the comfort of Hotel Chirag Haveli.

Gadisar Lake

Embark on a tranquil journey just a 5-minute stroll from our haveli, and you'll find yourself at the enchanting Gadisar Lake. This picturesque water reservoir mirrors the charm of Jaisalmer, offering a serene haven where time seems to stand still. Immerse yourself in the tranquility and witness captivating views that unfold like a canvas of natural beauty. Discover the timeless allure of Gadisar Lake, just steps away from the doors of Hotel Chirag Haveli.

Thar Heritage Museum

Step into the tapestry of Rajasthan's rich cultural heritage with a visit to the Thar Heritage Museum, conveniently located just a short 5-minute drive from Chirag Haveli. This museum is a treasure trove of artifacts, each telling a story that spans centuries and encapsulates the essence of the Thar Desert.

Jaslamair: Discovering Unseen Treasures

Jaslamair, a hidden gem, offers a captivating journey. Its resilient community thrives in a challenging dusty environment, showcasing human spirit

Explore rich cultural traditions, experience warm hospitality, and capture stunning photographs. Jaslamair invites you to discover the beauty that flourishes amidst adversity, making it a unique destination.
